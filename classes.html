Spinning/Cycling

A fun and challenging spinning class that will burn calories fast. Participants travel on flat roads, climb gradients, sprint and race - all in cooling darkness.. A fantastic 45 minute, cardio and endurance workout. Suitable for all levels of fitness. Attendants please ensure to bring:

water
hydration is essential, 2 liter bottle per training is required
Towel
to leave equipment in clean order after training
Fitness clothing
for obvious reasons
